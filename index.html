About KALEIDOLA

-Welcome to KALEIDOLA, a third-person action puzzle game that takes you on a journey through a kaleidoscope-inspired world. Go on a mind-bending adventure that challenges players to think creatively and adapt to each new environment. As you encounter roadblocks along the way, don't give up. Master the art of switching between 3D mode and Kaleidoscope mode to achieve your objectives. Unlock KALEIDOLA's power when a flashing object is on screen. With a simple press of a button, take control and watch the creativity unfold. Transform objects, paths, and obstacles to overcome any dead-ends that seem impossible to pass. KALEIDOLA is a testament to the love and creativity of indie developer, HAYATOSKIE. Immerse yourself in their colourful world, where every twist and turn reveals their commitment to puzzle innovation and originality.
